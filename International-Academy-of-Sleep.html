Dr Avi Weisfogel

Dr. Avi Weisfogel is a previous dentist and is presently working as the Owner of the International Academy of Sleep. He graduated from Rutgers University with a degree in biology and after that went on to the New York University College of Dentistry where he earned a Doctorate of Dental Surgery. In 1999, Dr. Weisfogel founded Old Bridge Dental Care, an oral workplace that he operated for more than 15 years. During his time running Old Bridge Dental Care, Dr. Weisfogel earned a variety of awards from the surrounding neighborhood, including the sought after 'Best Dentist' award.

While running this practice, Dr. Avi Weisfogel began for more information about sleep and sleeping conditions and how to treat them. As Dr. Weisfogel began to discover, there are a variety of innovative techniques and approaches that can be used to treat various sleep disorders. Eager to check out how he might make a distinction on the planet of sleep, Dr. Weisfogel established Healthy Heart Sleep. Through this company, he worked with medical professionals from across the globe to help them develop and manage sleep labs.
